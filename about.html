Welcome to the Bachelor Pool

Predict the order of finish of the before the season starts, using the information on the official ABC site.

As the season progresses, we calculate how closely your predictions compare to the actual order of finish.

Whoever’s rankings are closest to the actual order of finish wins the prize money and title belt.

To participate, fill out the  entry form  and submit $5 on Venmo before noon on the day of the season premier.
